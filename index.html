What Would Stop This From Working?

We know what usually goes wrong with new software. This system is designed to avoid those headaches from day one.

IT & Register Issues

Worry: Will this mess up our cash registers or network?
Reality: No. We only *read* your data; we never write or change anything in your system. There is zero risk to your registers.

Manager Buy-In

Worry: Our managers are too busy to learn a complicated new tool.
Reality: They don't have to. The system sends them a daily notification that takes 5 minutes to review and approve. It saves them time.

Spamming Customers

Worry: Will we annoy people with too many texts?
Reality: We put strict limits on how often we message people. A customer only gets a text if they are a perfect match for that specific event.

Keeping Data Safe

Reality: Yes. We use top-tier security. Customer information is scrambled and kept completely private for your store only.
